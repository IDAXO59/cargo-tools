How Location Update Works

Turns a ZIP, a City/State, or a map link into a ready-to-send team message with the location.

How to use
  1. 1Enter the current location: a 5-digit ZIP, a "City, ST", or a Google/Apple Maps link.
  2. 2Optionally add a Start and End point to draw the route on the map.
  3. 3Press Go — the location is resolved to a "City, ST ZIP" and shown on the mini map.
  4. 4Shortened Apple links (maps.apple/p/…) can't be read — send a Google Maps link or share your position instead.
  5. 5Press Copy and paste the message into your team chat.
