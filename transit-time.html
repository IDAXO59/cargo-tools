How Transit Time Works

Calculates realistic drive time using FMCSA Hours of Service (HOS) rules.

How to use
  1. 1Enter 5-digit origin and destination ZIP codes
  2. 2Coordinates fetched from Zippopotam.us, road distance from OSRM
  3. 3Average speed of 50 mph applied to total road miles
  4. 4After 8h continuous driving → mandatory 30-min break
  5. 5After 11h driving in a shift → 10h HOS rest period
  6. 6Every 500 miles → 30-min fuel stop (also resets continuous counter)
  7. 7All parameters can be adjusted in the Parameters section
